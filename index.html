Castles&TeaRooms

Reviews and adventures

Has anyone ever visited every single castle in Ireland?

I don't know but I've decided I want to. Whether it's a ruin or a fully functional tourable castle/mansion/rich persons own home, I'll document my adventures here. Along the way I'll drink as much tea as possible with the occasional castle themed cake or pastry in the accompanying teaRoom or café. If there's no teaRoom or café, I'll just have to bring a flask.
When visiting castles, you need tea. #NotAnAlternativeFact
